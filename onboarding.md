## Day 1 Schedule (General Flow)

| # | Activity | Details |
|---|----------|---------|
| 1 | **Team Introduction Meeting** | Meet with the development team you'll be working with. Informal intro to your teammates and their roles. |
| 2 | **HR & Payroll Paperwork** | Complete required employment documents and payroll setup with HR. |
| 3 | **Company Orientation** | 1:1 session covering company background, org structure, and mission/values. |
| 4 | **SproutHR Walkthrough** | Guided tour of the SproutHR system (see modules below). |
| 5 | **Laptop & Hardware Setup** | Receive and set up your company laptop and any other required hardware. |

### Company Orientation covers:
- Company background and what Candy Corner Philippines Inc. distributes
- General organizational structure (who reports to whom)
- Company mission and values
- Company Benefits
- Paid leave credits
- Org chart / employee directory

### SproutHR Walkthrough covers:
- Payroll
- CIAO
- Time off / leave requests
- Attendance
- My Requests
- Clock in / Clock out

## Tips for New Hires

- Take notes during the SproutHR walkthrough — there are several modules to remember (payroll, attendance, leave, official business).
- Don't hesitate to ask your dev team for help setting up your environment; it's expected to take some back-and-forth.
- Confirm with your manager what your first task or project will be if it isn't assigned on Day 1.
